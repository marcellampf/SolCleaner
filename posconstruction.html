Post-Construction Cleaning

Our Post-Construction Cleaning service is designed to handle the mess left behind after construction or renovation projects. We ensure your new or renovated space is spotless, safe, and ready for use.

Services Included:

  • Cleaning of all walls, doors, and baseboards
  • Intensive scrubbing of bathrooms and kitchens
  • Cleaning inside cabinets and upholstery
  • Thorough dusting and vacuuming
  • Deep cleaning of tough spots, such as the refrigerator and oven
  • Cleaning sockets and switches
  • Removing paint stains and other residues
