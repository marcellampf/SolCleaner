Airbnb Cleaning Services

Description

Ensure your Airbnb property is always guest-ready with our professional cleaning services. We specialize in providing thorough, high-quality cleanings that leave your space spotless and welcoming for new guests.

Our Services:

Complete Guest Turnover

  • Removal of Guest Belongings: We carefully remove all belongings left behind by the previous guest to prepare the space for new arrivals.

Deep Cleaning

  • Cabinets and Refrigerator: We perform a deep clean of all cabinets and the refrigerator, ensuring they are sanitized and free of any residue or odors.

Floor Care

  • Vacuuming: thoroughly vacuum all carpets and rugs to remove dirt, dust, and allergens.
  • Mopping: We mop all hard floor surfaces to ensure they are clean and sparkling.

Window and Mirror Cleaning

  • Glass Cleaning: We clean all windows and glass surfaces, removing smudges and fingerprints for a crystal-clear finish.
  • Mirror Polishing: We polish all mirrors to ensure they are streak-free and shining.

Dusting

  • Surface Dusting: We dust all surfaces, including shelves, furniture, and decorative items, to eliminate dust and allergens.
